People

Whisler is home to a wide variety of people from all around the world. In the winter you can meet people from all walks of life and nationalities, Whisler has a large foreign worker population mostly from Australia. The community puts a lot of emphasis on artisan creations and supports a wide variety of businesses from candle making to coffee shops. Whisler's culture as a party town has survived three decades. There is never a time of year when the locals do not want to celebrate, you can find many local bars and clubs in the town center packed with partygoers. The people of Whisler are some of the most laid-back you will find in British Columbia; the atmosphere is set by the skiers as a laidback chill environment.
