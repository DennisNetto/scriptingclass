Economy

Whisler economy is greatly based in the service industry, it's home to many hotels and suits. The economy is driven by tourism surpassing a billion dollars a year in revenue. The biggest employer of Whisler is the Whisler and Blackcomb joint venture that supports both mountains. Both mountains are run daily by an estimated staff of over 1400 people, this ranges from mechanics to ticket takers. Small businesses are a pivotal driving force for Whisler economy, with the tourist inflow Whisler can support any type of small business due to the wide range of visitors almost any idea will have customers. One of the most notable ones would be the fossil shop spatializing in fossils, at high prices this may be unprofitable in other settings.
