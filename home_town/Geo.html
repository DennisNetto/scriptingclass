Geography

Whislers lowlands are full of lush forests with a plethora of local wildlife, the winding mountain passages make unequally shaped lakes that are intertwined around the mountains. The highlands have large mountains with massive glaciers nested inside of them with massive snowpacks popular with tourists. On the outer edge the mountains go from sea level to eight hundred and eighty meters forming the sea to sky. At the center of Whisler holds a giant valley home to the peak-to-peak gondola connecting Whisler and black comb with a unique breathtaking view. Near the coast you can find vertical mountains that have a ninety-degree slope popular with climbers.
