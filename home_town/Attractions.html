Attractions

Whisler is a tourist town and as such has many attractions. The peak to peak is a gondola that spans over four kilometers with an extremely large crevasse making it one of the most impressive attractions Whisler has to offer manufactured in 2010. The long horn saloon is a bar in the middle of Whisler and Blackcomb said to be the favorite destination by some very influential people, Microsoft even nicknamed one of their OS versions after it. In late June you can explore snow melt and walk though massive snow piles forming an impressive maze. Whisler also caters to the extreme hikers with many trails at high altitudes. Overall, the most popular attraction in Whisler is skiing.
